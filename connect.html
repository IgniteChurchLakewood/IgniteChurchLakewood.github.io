CONNECT

THERE'S A PLACE FOR EVERYONE TO CONNECT. 

Kids
We put a lot of energy into creating safe and fun environments where kids can learn about Jesus! It’s a place where faith, imagination, and learning go hand-in-hand. It’s a place where loving God is cool!

We think church should be fun for kids, so we’ve created experiences that are a blast for children of all ages! Your kids will participate in fun activities and crafts, energetic worship, and storytelling with other children their age.
Youth
We believe the next generation is the future of the church and is going to change the world! Our youth environments are designed to give them a place where they can ask honest questions, connect with Jesus, and find a community of faith with other students.
Life Groups
We have been designed to do life together. No one is meant to go it alone. This is where LifeGroups come in. These groups are where you connect and build a community, a support system. Within Ignite Church at Lakewood we have a variety of LifeGroups. We invite you to connect with a group that fits your life.
Ignite Men
Iron sharpens iron! Our men meet periodically to encourage healthy relationships between men that promote accountability and encourages spiritual growth. Men all have similar challenges as fathers, husbands, and providers regardless of where we are in our relationship with Christ. We need to know we don’t have to face struggles alone.
